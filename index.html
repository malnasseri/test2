Starvo Community

The StarVo community gives members who missed the chance to build a large audience on YouTube the power to create their personal brand and connect with like-minded creative souls to build a dedicated, passionate community around their story. Everyone has a talent even though they might not realize it. Our Community allows members to leave their comfort zone and express their talents.

Our users will have the opportunity to create 1-minute commercials showcasing those talents and also be able to create and share commercials on small businesses they shop at and love. StarVo also offers unique features not found on other platforms including our rating system that shows how users truly feel on individual posts and on overall profiles as well.

A team focused on helping small businesses!m

StarVo started with the realization that out of the 30,000,000 small businesses in our country right now, less than a third will make it past the ten year mark.

76% of those small business owners report significant challenges in marketing. Big corporations can afford the thousands to millions of dollars in marketing needed to keep the business rolling in and that makes it difficult for small businesses to compete.

Content marketing typically generates three times more leads than conventional marketing, however only about 46% of these small businesses have a website and less than 15% report using social media.

When 88% of consumers check online for reviews and information on a business before making a purchase or visiting a store, too many small businesses are simply going unseen.

It's time someone steps up and provides simple, effective and affordable tools to help small businesses begin to thrive!

So after months of research, hours upon hours of brainstorming and countless conversations, a team of passionate and creative people has been born. And this team has discovered a way to bring a powerful voice to the small businesses that we all love.
